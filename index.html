I’m a software engineer and game developer passionate about blending technical precision with captivating visuals. In the past, I’ve collaborated on projects ranging from educational technology to creative gaming systems. I also enjoy solving complex technical challenges, such as rendering real-time graphics, improving UI/UX experiences, and integrating seamless backend systems.
