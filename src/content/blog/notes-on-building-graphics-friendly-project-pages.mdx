Graphics-heavy work does not read well when it is flattened into a rectangular thumbnail and two bullets. The project page needs room for motion studies, implementation notes, and what the experiment was trying to learn.

The practical rule is simple: show the artifact, explain the system, and mention the decision that made the result possible.
